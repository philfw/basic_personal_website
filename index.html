ABOUT ME

My name is Phil Weiss. I am a NY-based attorney who represents startups and creative agencies, and I am an aspiring developer. I specialize in intellectual property and technology contracts. I am a Junior Partner at Fridman Law Group, I am an Adjunct Professor of CUBE at Brooklyn Law School, and I am a founder and one of the lead New York organizers for Legal Hackers.

My expertise is in copyright, trademark, corporate, and service contracts. I am a law nerd.

I also like gaming and technology. I live in Brooklyn. Sometimes for fun I will DJ a party.
